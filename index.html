Why Choose

Solar Energy in Florida?

Tax Breaks Have you heard of the Federal Investment Tax Credit (ITC)? Did you know that it expires at the end of 2021? Going solar this year can save you 26% on your total system cost. Do not hesitate before it's too late.
Pay $0 Down Start saving on your electricity costs today without having to worry about a down payment. There are many solar loans and financing options slated to help homeowners easily invest in clean, renewable energy.
Buyback Program (Net Metering) With solar energy, the eclectic utility company will actually pay you for the excess energy produced by your solar panels. Talk about a role reversal! In this sense, solar energy is a great way to earn passive income.
Increased Home Value Other than a front door, a renewable energy system is one of the best ways to increase the value of your home. In fact, according to a 2019 nationwide study from Zillow, homes with solar panels sell for 4.1% more than homes without. Solar energy systems eliminate electricity costs, making your home more valuable.
Long Term Savings Have you ever noticed how your utility’s electricity billing rate rarely decreases? Protect yourself against future rate increases and decrease your grid dependence by producing solar energy, right on your property.
For the Environment If you are worried about climate change, then installing solar panels is a great way to play your part for the environment. Every kWh of electricity produced by solar energy (compared to coal) saves 700g of carbon emissions from entering the Earth’s atmosphere.
